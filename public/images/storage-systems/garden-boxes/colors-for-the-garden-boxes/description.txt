Colors for the garden boxes
//

1) Standard colour outside: grey aluminium

The standard colour grey aluminium RAL 9007 can be chosen for the outer colour design.


2) Standard colour outside: anthracite grey

The standard colour anthracite grey RAL 7016 can be chosen for the outer colour design.


3) Standard colour inside: Grey white

The interior is designed in the colour grey-white RAL 9002.

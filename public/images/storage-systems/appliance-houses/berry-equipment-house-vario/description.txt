 Berry equipment house Vario

With the Vario motif, the outside of the Berry equipment house can be individually designed, because it enables on-site planking. Choose your desired filling to realize your personal design ideas and adapt the equipment house to the garden style. Hörmann provides the frame construction for the later on-site planking, as standard in black or on request in a freely selectable RAL colour. The Vario motif is available for both roof shapes – plyc and gable roof – as well as in three different sizes.

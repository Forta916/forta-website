
For a more sustainable future – CO2-neutral components

Standard for housing, optionally for contract construction

//

As a family business, we are aware of the responsibility for future generations and offer our products for housing as standard CO 2 -neutral at all European sales locations. At customer request, our products for contract construction are optionally available in Europe CO2-neutral. This means that the purchase decision will take over the compensation costs for remaining emissions and thus actively contribute. With its sustainability strategy, Hörmann is pursuing the goal of reducing and avoiding emissions. We source 100% green electricity from renewable energies for our entire electricity requirements at all European production sites. In addition, we reduce our consumption through many other measures and save more than 75,000 tonnes of CO 2 annually. We compensate for the remaining emissions by promoting certified climate protection projects in cooperation with ClimatePartner.

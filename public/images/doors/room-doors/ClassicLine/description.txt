
ClassicLine

For lovers of classic and elegance

//

Do you like it classic, but still modern? Then our timeless motifs of the ClassicLine are the right choice for you. For the perfect country house style you can now discover your favourite door from the ClassicLine.

All motifs of this series convince with craftsmanship details in the best joinery quality:

The particularly high-quality Georgia doors Georgia with its inlaid profile strip. The motifs Carolina with embossed profiling, the Montana motifs with high-quality refurbished frames or the motifs of Kontura with contour-milled profiles.

For individual colour design, almost all doors of the ClassicLine with a lacquer surface can be designed in RAL of your choice.

In addition, our door models of the ClassicLine are also available in matt versions in 7 preferred colours. 


1) ClassicLine Montana 5 Color: Traffic white RAL 9016

2) ClassicLine Kontura 3 Color: Traffic white RAL 9016

3) ClassicLine Carolina 2 Color: Traffic white RAL 9016

4) ClassicLine Carolina 1 Color: Traffic white RAL 9016

5) ClassicLine Montana 4 Color: Traffic white RAL 9016

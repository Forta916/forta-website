
Aluminum doors ThermoCarbon

ThermoCarbon doors are a premium class among aluminium entrance doors. They are absolutely leaders in terms of thermal insulation and even certified for passive houses. In addition, they meet the highest requirements in terms of burglary protection with standard RC3 and optional RC4 safety equipment. The door leaf is 100 mm thick and reinforced with a carbon fiber optical hybrid material. This allows a particularly large selection of motifs and exclusive designs such as e.g. embedded handles for a flush door view.

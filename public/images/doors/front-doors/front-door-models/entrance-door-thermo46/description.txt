
Thermo46

Make your entrance to the eye-catcher

//

The Thermo46 doors offer you everything that makes a good entrance door. With UD values of up to 1.1 W/(m2-K), the Thermo46 doors achieve good thermal insulation values. Thanks to the standard 5-fold security lock, the entrance doors are ideally equipped in terms of security. Optionally, they can also be equipped with a self-locking lock. Because it is automatically locked when closing the door, manual closing with a key is no longer necessary and at the same time the insurance cover is always given during attempts to break-in – practical! Some motifs of the Thermo46 doors are optionally available with the RC2 safety equipment for an even more comfortable feeling in your own home.

Choose your favourite from – from modern to classic. With colours, glazing, side panels and skylights, you can customize your Hörmann door to your taste.

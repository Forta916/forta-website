
Steel / aluminum front doors Thermo65

The doorsteps Thermo65 are a cheaper alternative to our aluminum front doors. They are made of a high-quality material combination with a steel door leaf and a frame made of aluminium. The 65 mm thick door leaf with standard 5-fold safety lock and optional RC2 equipment is burglar-resistant and offers good thermal insulation values. In addition, there are a variety of individual design options. Whether a modern or more classic motif, with or without glazing – with us you will find the front door that best suits your wishes and your home. Our high-quality steel / aluminium front door Thermo65 is also available in 2-cular design and thus creates an even larger through width for e.g. B. Stroller, wheelchairs or furniture. The standing wing can also be chosen narrower than the aisle wing: dimensions from 700 mm are possible. The flush transition from aisle and stand wing ensures a high-quality and modern appearance of the front door.

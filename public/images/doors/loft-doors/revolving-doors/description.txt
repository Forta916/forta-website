Revolving doors

The classic variant : revolving doors are anchored in the door frame with hinges and can be opened inside and outwards. The ideal solution for a stylish feeling of space with that certain extra.

//

1) 1-Single-leaf swing door made of steel and glass 
Are you looking for a classic door with large glazing and modern steel profiles? Then use a 1-ly-lythaled revolving wing door from Hörmann to provide more light and transparency in the living room and set a stylish and modern accent.

    1-flügly revolving leaf doors are available for smaller passages from a width of 650 mm and a height of 1900 mm

2) 2-Leaf swing door made of steel and glass 
Do you love generous passageways and openness as well as clear structures? Then our 2-wing revolving leaf doors are exactly the right solution for you. A 2-wing loft door creates a unique feeling of space and is a real eye-catcher in your home. With large openings, the doors with two door wings separate rooms from each other and at the same time offer transparency and a generous passageway. This is e.g. especially practical between living room and kitchen or the transition from living area and hallway.

    2-flying loft doors made of steel and glass are already available from a width of 1250 mm and a height of 1900 mm
    The largest Hörmann Loft door with two door wings is 2500 mm wide and 3000 mm high
    With supplementary side panels and skylights, even larger elements are possible, which close particularly large openings and further underline the modern industrial look of the doors

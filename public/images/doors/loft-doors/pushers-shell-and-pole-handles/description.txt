
Pushers, shell and pole handles

For an optimally coordinated design

//

Round off the industrial style of Hörmann loft doors with the right door handle or a modern shell or pole handle. In total, between 11 handle sets with and without lock as well as 4 shell handles and 11 rod handles can be selected.

The handle sets are made as standard in deep black RAL 9005 or in stainless steel on request. The Kome D-810 pusher can be ordered in stainless steel, raw stainless steel or brass. The narrow profile width of the pushers underlines the delicate, modern view of the loft doors. For a particularly minimalist design, the pushers are executed without a rosette. If you need a lock for your loft door, it can also be optionally equipped with a single-tube frame lock with profile cylinder.

The shell handles provide a modern and minimalist design and are available in deep black RAL 9005. Bar handles place a special emphasis on the door leaf and underline the clear structure of the loft doors. In total, you can choose from 11 different models, which are adapted to the respective door colour in the colour. The length of the HL G-06 bar handles is dependent on the lamination layout and is individually matched to your door. The HL G-07 rod handles to HL G-09 can be made in a length of 300 mm to 1100 mm. The modern and high-quality rod handle models HL G-10 to HL G-16 can be ordered with a length of 500 mm.

An e-opener, an electronic locking system and a mechanical slot lock for bar handles are optionally available for convenient access control. In addition, the steel loft doors with a toilet profile cylinder and frosted glass panes can now also be used as e.g. B. Bathroom door can be used.


ZK multi-purpose steel door

Robust, dimensionally stable and hard-wearing


//

The ZK multi-purpose steel door is robust and dimensionally stable and therefore ideally suited as an indoor or outdoor door for basement, garages and outbuildings. In addition, the door impresses with good thermal insulation, quick installation and an attractive price-performance ratio. The double-walled, 40 mm thick door leaf with a sheet thickness of 0.8 mm ensures a high stability of the door. The 3-sided thick rebate version creates an elegant door view, which harmonises particularly well with barreled interior doors. Furthermore, the door convinces with high energy efficiency: The steel door leaf, which is fully glued together with a styrofoam insert, ensures good thermal insulation with a UD value of up to 1.6 W/(m2-K), below the limit value of the Building Energy Act (1.8 W/m2 K). The steel door is supplied as standard with a corner frame with 3-sided seal, wall anchor and floor holder as well as with mortise lock with steel trap and maintenance-free, 3-piece strips V0026 WF. The safety bolt on the hinge side ensures a high level of safety and protects against uninvited guests. In addition, the completely ready-to-install door element allows quick and easy installation. For more light incidence, glazing in the door leaf is also possible on request. For use as an outside door, the steel door can optionally be equipped with a lowerable floor seal, an aluminium impact profile with seal and a slip-on aluminium strip.

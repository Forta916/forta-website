The fire protection door FS Thermo65 is tested in retardant after El 2 30 and can withstand for up to 30 minutes in case of fire. In addition, the door with a 65 mm thick door leaf offers very good thermal insulation and reaches a UD value of up to 0.85 W/(m2-K). This is significantly below the limit value of the Building Energy Act (1.8 W/(m2-K). Thanks to thermal separation, the door offers up to 49% better thermal insulation compared to a conventional fire protection door. In addition, the risk of sweating water on the door leaf is reduced in heated rooms. In combination with the motif 610S, the FS Thermo65 with frameless glazing cut-out and clear or frosted satin-finish glazing ensures a modern door design and natural light incidence. With the standard top door closer, the fire protection door can be opened and closed comfortably. The smokedness S 200 is achieved exclusively with a multi-point locking system.

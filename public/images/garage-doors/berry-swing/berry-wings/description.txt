Everything speaks for the original: quality and safety, appearance and comfort and the sophisticated technology. Every Hörmann Berry-Tor contains the experience of more than 50 years of know-how in the construction of garage gates. No matter for which a Berry swingbar style you are looking for, at Hörmann you will certainly find the gate motif that suits you and your house – from straightforward beads to classic cassettes in many finishes, colours and decors, and as a solid wood gate. With almost every torture, Hörmann also provides you with the right side door. Thus the gate and the door form a harmonious unity.

Berry wings-goers are tested and certified according to the high safety requirements of the European standard 13241. You can check with the safety checklist whether your old garage door meets the modern safety standards.

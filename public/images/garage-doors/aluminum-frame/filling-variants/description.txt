
Filling variants

From clearly to tinted to breathable

//

When glazing the Hörmann aluminum frame doors, you have the choice between different filling variants.




1) Polycarbonate double disc, clear or plastic double lens, clear

2) Plastic double disc, crystal structure

3) Bridge double plate

4) Plastic double disc, white tinted (opal)

5) Plastic double disc, brown tinted

6) Plastic double disc, grey tinted

7) PU sandwich filling, smooth

8) PU sandwich filling, stucco

9) Wave grid

10) Perforated stainless steel

11) Stretch grille made of stainless steel

12) Welded grilles
